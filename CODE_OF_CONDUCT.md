# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project maintainers.

For more details see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
